The system designed for YOUR safety.

SocialGuard is a centralized Trustlist designed to keep your server safe from known intruders (such as Raiders, Trolls, Chasers, Scammers, Bots, etc).

An immediate solution to an immediate threat.

As malicious events happen so fast, any prevention solution requires an immediate propagation.

SocialGuard has no propagation delay. As our API centralises all results, all entries by our emitters are effective immediately.

An Open(-Source) System.

SocialGuard is open to use by anyone, and with public records.

As we operate with transparency, all our records are public, and accessible with our API. This allows any developer to integrate SocialGuard's records into their project, and servers. Moreover, our entire project is Open-Source, and its development is community-driven. You can inspect our code on our GitHub Repo.
